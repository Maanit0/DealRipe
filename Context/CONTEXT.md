C1/Competition: Do we know who else might be bidding?
O1/Originality: Can we offer something original or unique?
T1/Timescale: Are we aware of their timescales?
T2/Timescale: Is the timescale defined?
T3/Timescale: Does it fall within our capability?
S1/Size: Is the project size worth pursuing?
S2/Size: Is the client of a size that makes it viable?
M1/Money: Is a target budget defined?
M2/Money: How much is it? Is it approved?

A2/Authority: Do we know who has authority to decide?
A3/Authority: Do we have access to the decision maker?
A4/Authority: Do we know who else is involved?
N1/Need: Our solution aligns to the desired outcome
N2/Need: Can we match all identified needs?
